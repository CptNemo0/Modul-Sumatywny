How to play?

Gamepads:

Movement: Left stick
Choke: Hold both triggers
Cast: Square (left button)
Interact / Pickup: Square (left button)

If you are alone / don't have gamepads:

Player 1: 
Movement: FGHT 
Choke: Y
Cast: R
Interact / Pickup: E

Player 2: 
Movement: JKLI 
Choke: U
Cast: O

When interacting with menus:
Arrows + ENTER
